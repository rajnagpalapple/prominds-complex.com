Promind Complex Ingredients 

ProMind Complex is formulated with a selection of natural ingredients known for their cognitive-enhancing properties. The ingredients used in the formation of Promind Complexare mentioned below:

1

Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo Biloba is a widely recognized herb that has been used for centuries to improve blood circulation and cognitive function. It's known for its antioxidant properties and its ability to support brain health and memory.
2

Vinpocetine

Vinpocetine, derived from the periwinkle plant, is used to enhance memory and cognition. It is believed to increase blood flow to the brain and has neuroprotective effects.
3

Phosphatidylserine

Phosphatidylserine is a phospholipid that plays a crucial role in cell membrane integrity and is vital for efficient neurotransmission. It's often used to improve attention, language skills, and memory.
4

Huperzine

Huperzine is an extract from the Chinese club moss plant, touted for its ability to improve memory and mental function, particularly in Alzheimer's disease, by increasing levels of neurotransmitters.
5

Acetyl-L-carnitine

Acetyl-L-carnitine is an amino acid that is naturally produced in the body and supports energy production. It is linked to improvements in alertness, memory, and cognitive function.
6

St. John's Wort

St. John's Wort is a plant that has been used for centuries to treat mental health conditions. It is commonly utilized for its potential to alleviate depression and stabilize mood.
7

Bacopa monnieri

Bacopa monnieri, also known as Brahmi, is an herb traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to enhance brain function, improve memory and reduce stress-induced anxiety.
8

N-Acetyl-L-Tyrosine

N-Acetyl-L-Tyrosine is an amino acid that the body converts into substances that are essential for brain health and neurotransmitter production, influencing cognitive processes.
9

L-Glutamine

L-Glutamine is an amino acid that serves as a building block for protein in the body and is also a precursor to the neurotransmitter glutamate, involved in cognitive functions.
10

B-Complex vitamins

B-Complex vitamins are a group of water-soluble vitamins that play important roles in cell metabolism and energy production, with several of them directly impacting brain health and cognitive function.
