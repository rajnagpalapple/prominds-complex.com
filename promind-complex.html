**Phosphatidylserine**: This ingredient plays a crucial role in the proper functioning of nerve cells and is involved in the clotting function of the body. Being a fatty substance, it nourishes brain cell membranes and produces cell-renewing nutrients.

Huperzine

**Huperzine**: Derived from the moss Huperzia serrata, Huperzine is considered a potential remedy for Alzheimer’s disease. It works by improving neurotransmitter levels in the brain, reducing inflammation caused by plaque or bacteria, and blocking bacterial invasion.

Bacopa monnieri

**Bacopa monnieri**: A traditional herb, Bacopa monnieri is often used in nootropic supplements for its potential cognitive-enhancing properties. It's believed to support memory, focus, and overall brain health.

St. John’s Wort

**St. John’s Wort**: Known for its mood-enhancing properties, St. John’s Wort is often used to treat depression and mood disorders. It's believed to have a positive effect on neurotransmitter activity, promoting mental well-being.

N-Acetyl-L-Carnitine
